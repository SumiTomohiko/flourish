Flourish

Description

Flourish is an action game, with strong inspiration from Brandish for Nippon Falcom.

You need a mouse to play.

Story

One dwarf fell to a dungeon. The dwarf started to find the way to the ground.

Screenshots

Demo

Requirements

  • Ubuntu Linux 8.04
  • Yog 0.0.10
  • Intel Pentium M 1.4GHz or more

How to Play

How to Start

Run flourish.

$ flourish

Title Screen

Click "Start" to start a game. Click "Quit" to close the window.

Main Screen

A character on the bottom side is you.

How to Move

You can move by clicking inside of squares around you.

How to Attack

If an enemy is in front of you, you can click the inside of the square in front of you to attack the enemy.

Life

You can see your life at the upper left part in the main screen. If it becomes zero, you will die.

Author

The author of Flourish is SumiTomohiko.
